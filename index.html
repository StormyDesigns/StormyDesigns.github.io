About

I'm a young creative from Anaheim, California currently working as a graphic designer at the University of Southern California as well as pursuing a B.F.A. in Design there. I take inspiration from everyday life and incorporate it into my designs.

I'm a visual storyteller, a crafter of interfaces and someone who brings imagination to life, a future unicorn, a learner, a dreamer - and me. When I'm not designing, I'm traveling, taking photos of everything I find beautiful, and spending time with the ones I love.

Baked4Good

Description

My group was assigned to identify a cause or non-profit hat is relevant to our community. We had to plan a fund-raising event to raise money to support that cause. In addition, our group pitched our event concept to the class using both printed and projected media.

Our group decided to create Baked 4 Good, a bake sale of marijuana edibles that will take place on 4/20 to help raise funds for the Chan Zuckerberg initiative. The proceeds of the bake sale will be dedicated towards helping the decriminalization of people charged for the possession of marijuana through the Chan Zuckerberg Foundation. The money will go towards hiring attorneys to review cases and defend those charged.

My main roles were to create the presentation, pitch deck, and some illustrations.
